5 Major Climatic Types

Tropical moist climates

All months have an average temperature above 18°C (64°F). Since all months are warm, there is no real winter season.

Dry climates

Deficient precipitation most of the year. Potential evaporation and transpiration exceed precipitation.

Moist mid-latitude with mild winters

Warm-to-hot summers with mild winters. The average temperature of the coldest month is below 18°C (64°F) and above ‒3°C (27°F).

Moist mid-latitude with severe winters

Warm summers and cold winters. The average the temperature of the warmest month exceeds 10°C (50°F), and the coldest monthly average drops below ‒3°C (27°F).

Polar climates

Extremely cold winters and summers. The average temperature of the warmest month is below 10°C (50°F). Since all months are cold, there is no real summer season.
